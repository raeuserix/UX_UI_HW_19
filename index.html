Hi there, I'm Rachel!

I am an emerging UI/UX designer whose passion for design comes from rooting out the source of a problem and looking for creative, user-friendly, and intuitive solutions that will help a product excel to its fullest potential.


My strengths include prototyping and wireframing mobile applications and responsive websites, conducting user research and rooting out existing pain points, and communicating with my clients and teammates to ensure the users' goals are met.

MY SKILLS

WIREFRAMING AND PROTOTYPING

Using online design tools like Figma, I am well-versed in prototyping wireframes in order to visually represent one or more user flows for mobile applications or website designs.

USER EMPATHY AND
RESEARCH

Empathizing with users and understanding their needs are of the utmost importance when creating or iterating products. My goal is to listen and root out the core problems that they face today with products via user interviews and data analysis.

COLLABORATION AND COMMUNICATION

I value the time I spend working with my teammates and clients to further understand user goals and come up with design/usability solutions.

I am always open to constructive feedback from others (I frequently provide said feedback as well) and always find myself learning something new every day!
